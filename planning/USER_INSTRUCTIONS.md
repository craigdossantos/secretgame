### ğŸ”“ Unlocking Secrets

**Why Are Secrets Locked?**
Secrets are blurred until you unlock them. This creates a fair trade system.

**How to Unlock:**
1. Find a locked secret (e.g., Level 3)
2. Click **"Unlock"**
3. You'll be asked to share a secret of the same or higher level
4. Once you submit your secret, the locked one reveals!

**Example:**
- Alice shared a Level 4 secret
- To see it, you must share a Level 4+ secret
- This ensures everyone contributes to unlock
